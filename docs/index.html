Privacy Policy for Nudge

Last updated: February 6, 2025

Introduction

Nudge ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address, display name, and profile picture (if provided via Google Sign-In).

User Content: We store reminders, tasks, and shared spaces that you create within the app.

Device Information: We collect device tokens for push notification delivery.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the app's functionality
  • To send push notifications for reminders you've set
  • To enable shared spaces and collaboration features
  • To authenticate your account

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase services, which comply with industry-standard security practices. Data is stored in secure cloud servers with encryption in transit and at rest.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Your data may be shared only:

  • With other users you explicitly invite to shared spaces
  • With service providers (Firebase/Google) for app functionality
  • If required by law

Your Rights

You can:

  • Access your data through the app
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt out of notifications through device settings

Children's Privacy

Nudge is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
